Section 3

I believe that our ability to generate/consume content is easier than it has ever been, in fact maybe too easy. However, I do believe that there are still difficulties that exist within content consumption. One aspect I find difficult is that there are so many companies that split rights to the same sort of media. I believe this issue is most prevelant when trying to stream sports. For football alone: Amazon owns rights to NFL Thursday Night Football, CBS, Fox, and NBC split games on Sunday, and ESPN own Monday Night Football- and that's jsut for the NFL! I do think that competition amongst providers is better than monopoly, and there are more costly services like cable and NFL Gamepass that allow acces to all games, but I believe this aspect still results in difficulties for consumers.
